How Sahayak Works

Our simple three-step process ensures complete transparency and security for both requesters and donors

1

Aadhaar Verification

Every user verifies their identity using Aadhaar to ensure complete transparency and build trust within our community.

2

Create or Browse Requests

Request financial help during emergencies or browse verified requests categorized by need to make meaningful contributions.

3

Secure UPI Donations

Donate securely via UPI with complete transaction transparency. Every rupee is tracked and accounted for.

About Sahayak

Our mission is to create India's most trusted crowdfunding ecosystem

Sahayak is a secure, Aadhaar-authenticated crowdfunding platform where verified users can raise financial requests during emergencies. Unlike other platforms, we prioritize transparency and accountability through identity verification and detailed tracking of fund utilization.

Our platform operates as a SaaS model, offering admin access to NGOs and super-admin control to platform owners, ensuring complete transparency and accountability in the crowdfunding process. We partner with banks and UPI providers to facilitate seamless, secure transactions.
